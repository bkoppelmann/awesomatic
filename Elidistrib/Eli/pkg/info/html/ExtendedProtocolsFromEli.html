    Three files containing information about
    • grammar symbols
    • associated attributes
    • rules and computations

  • foo.specs : showMe : viewlist
    Three files containing information about
    • grammar symbols
    • associated attributes, after remote access expansion
    • rules and scheduled computations

  • foo.specs : ExpInfo>
    Information about remote access expansion (Chains, Constituents and Includings)

  • foo.specs : OrdInfo>
    Information about attribute dependences and (if available) generated tree visit sequences
